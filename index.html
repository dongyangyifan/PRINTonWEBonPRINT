Dear Audience,

Every day ☀︎ and night ★, we would look at something on some surface. Paper and screen are the two dominant and prevailing surface in my 2017 mundane life.

This website and its print exhibits is a short story of what happened when the standard size paper exists in real scale on the screen within the browser viewport, and the browser sizes being printed in real scale on standard size paper within their crop marks.

With the paper only partly showed up on the screen condition, the online exhibition reminds us the browser sizes as well as its limitation. While we print from online, we got the browser sizes being print on paper to tell us the volatility of its form.

Paper and screen reveal each other in this little interaction. The screen is much limited with incredible potential, while the paper is the same.

Feel the scale of the paper in the analog scenario and look at the inch numbers while we dive in the virtual world. Through the two corresponding formats and content, the exhibition is for this collective experience we have now when wandering in between print and web.

If you have to know more, Google “paper sizes” or “browser sizes”. Look what I found when I just Google:

papersizes.io/us/
apple.com/mac/compare/
apple.com/ipad/compare/

Thank you!

Yours,
The person who takes full responsibility of the whole experience: Yangyifan Dong
